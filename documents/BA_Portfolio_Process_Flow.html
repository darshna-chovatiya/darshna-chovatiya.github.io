Dvik Jewels - Etsy Store Operations

1
Order Received
Customer places order on Etsy platform. Order notification received via email with complete order details including product, quantity, shipping address, and payment confirmation.
System / Customer
2
Order Validation
Review order details for accuracy, verify payment status, and check for any special customer requests or customization requirements.
Store Manager
3
Inventory Check & Supplier Coordination
Verify product availability with supplier. If in stock, reserve item for order. If out of stock, coordinate with supplier for restocking timeline and communicate expected delivery date to customer.
Store Manager / Supplier
4
Product Procurement
Receive product from supplier. Conduct quality inspection to ensure item meets standards. Check for defects, verify specifications match order requirements.
Store Manager
5
Packaging & Labeling
Carefully package item with protective materials. Create professional packaging with branded materials. Print and attach shipping label with accurate address information.
Store Manager
6
Shipping Handoff
Coordinate with logistics partner (USPS, UPS, FedEx) for package pickup or drop-off. Obtain tracking number and update order status in Etsy system.
Store Manager / Logistics Partner
7
Customer Communication
Send shipping confirmation email to customer with tracking information. Provide estimated delivery date and respond to any customer inquiries throughout shipping process.
Store Manager
8
Delivery & Follow-up
Monitor tracking for successful delivery. Send post-delivery follow-up email requesting feedback and review. Address any customer concerns or issues promptly.
Store Manager / Customer
